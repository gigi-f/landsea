# Landsea Controls

## Movement & Navigation
- **W / A / S / D**: Move Player
- **E**: Interact
  - **Steer Boat**: Press E near the Rudder to take control.
  - **Use Boat Parts**: Press E near Sail/Anchor to toggle them.
- **K**: Kick Off (Push boat away from land if stuck)

## Combat & Harvesting
- **Space**: Attack / Harvest
  - Swing your knife to chop trees, break rocks, or clear bushes.
  - Different resources have different hardness (Trees take multiple hits).

## Survival & Inventory
- **I** or **Tab**: Toggle Inventory
- **C**: Consume Food/Water (Prioritizes Thirst/Hunger needs)
- **B**: Toggle Crafting Menu
  - Press **Number Keys (1-9)** to craft items when menu is open.

## Building & Placement
- **P**: Place / Build
  - **Build Boat**: Hold a **Plank** and press P while facing the ocean (adjacent to existing boat) to expand the hull.
  - **Place Campfire**: Hold a **Campfire** and press P while standing on Land to place it.

## Debug / Admin
- **F3**: Toggle Debug Info (if implemented)
